How to Play

Player 1 Controls:
- Move: A / D
- Jump: W
- Fireball: Q
- Gun Shoot: F
- Reload: R
- Activate Shield: E
- Punch: V
- Bomb: B
- Punish: Z
- Sword Slash: C
- Laser: X
- Boomerang: G
- Freeze Ray: T
- Heal: Y

Player 2 Controls:
- Move: ← / →
- Jump: ↑
- Fireball: /
- Gun Shoot: ;
- Reload: '
- Activate Shield: .
- Punch: [
- Bomb: P
- Punish: L
- Sword Slash: M
- Laser: K
- Boomerang: H
- Freeze Ray: U
- Heal: O

Game Rules:
- Each player has 600 health
- Use weapons to damage your opponent
- Shields protect for 10s with cooldown
- First to deplete the opponent’s health wins
